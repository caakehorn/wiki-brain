# Psychosexual Profile

The corpus's own diagnostic language names sexuality as one of Dan's central
organizing systems, not a peripheral trait: the August 2025 deviance audit
scores the "psychosexual operating system" a 96/100 outlier with 90%
confidence — the fourth-most-unusual thing about him out of ten ranked
domains ([[wiki/mind/profile/deviance-mapping]]) — and a separate,
fuller AI-authored report devotes an entire section to it under the
heading "The Psychosexual Crucible: Erotics as Mythogenesis." Both sources
converge on the same claim, restated across the corpus in nearly identical
language: sex functions for Dan as a **meaning-generating system**, not a
recreational one — "sex is his church" — organized around orchestration
rather than participation, edge-testing rather than transgression for its
own sake, and total emotional absorption rather than casual encounter. This
hub indexes both the theoretical framework (mostly single-source,
AI-narrativized, and flagged as such throughout) and the decade of
documented real-world instances that give it primary-source grounding.

## LLM Quick Brief

**For context injection:** Dan's sexuality is documented as an "engineered,"
non-casual system rather than incidental behavior. Three named mechanisms
recur across sources: (1) **orchestration/voyeurism** — arousal from
constructing and witnessing a scenario rather than direct participation,
paired with a self-reported total absence of reciprocity (Annie has slept
with other people in Dan's presence across a decade; the reverse is
undocumented); (2) **taboo as boundary-testing**, not shock-seeking — the
same edge-condition-probing pattern documented in his cognitive style
([[wiki/mind/profile/enneagram-5w4]]) applied to sexual/social categories;
(3) **total emotional imprinting** — every high-investment encounter is
described as triggering immediate attachment activation, framed as the
mechanism behind both relationship intensity and betrayal severity (the
[[wiki/people/eli|Eli]] incident as "temple desecration," not disappointment).
The theory is single-source and AI-authored; the *practice* has a decade of
primary corpus support: the smashonista camming era, the Trinity St. Clair
and Kelly Johansson paid bookings, the Shelbie Breakiron and Annie/Alexis
threesome-adjacent events, and the Kristin "purity test" architecture are
all independently documented in message threads, not just described in
retrospective analysis. Detail pages: [[wiki/mind/psychosexual/orchestration-and-voyeurism]],

## How to read this cluster

The same three provenance caveats that govern [[wiki/mind/profile/index]]
apply here, with one addition specific to this material. First, the
theoretical framework — the "recursive mythogenesis engine," "erotic
control architecture," "psychosexual inversion" language — comes from a
small number of AI-authored dossiers Dan commissioned and co-wrote
(`Dan Profile.txt`'s "MASTER ANALYSIS," the deviance audit), not from
independent clinical or third-party assessment; the vivid metaphor
("temple desecration," "Judas Contract," "cosmic irony") is interpretive
coloring layered onto real events, not a separate factual claim. Second,
several specific claims in the theoretical layer — the "Danny" massage
incident, the timing-coincidence with [[wiki/people/fran-coldren|Fran]]'s
1918 injury — have **no corroboration anywhere else in the corpus** and in
at least one case conflict with a better-sourced date already established
elsewhere in the wiki (see [[wiki/mind/psychosexual/orchestration-and-voyeurism]]
for the specific contradiction). Treat single-sourced claims as a framework
worth keeping on file, not verified biography. Third — and this is what
distinguishes this cluster from the rest of [[wiki/mind/profile/index]] —
the *practice* layer is unusually well corroborated: unlike the typology
scores, which rest on self-report and AI inference, the arrangement
history in [[wiki/mind/psychosexual/arrangement-history]] is built almost
entirely from primary iMessage/SMS threads with named people, dates, and
direct quotes, cross-checked against the corpus's own documented
direction-field unreliability (see [[wiki/self/message-corpora/master-message-dump]]).
The theory and the practice should be read as separate evidentiary tiers,
not a single unified fact set.
