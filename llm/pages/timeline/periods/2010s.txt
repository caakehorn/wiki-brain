# NYC Round One (2010–2013)

Dan's first New York City period ran **April 2010 to May 2013** — three
years, two apartments, the audio-engineering career attempt, and the
first long cohabitation of his adult life, with [[wiki/people/alexis-armel|Alexis]].
It opens off the [[wiki/timeline/periods/full-sail-2008-2010|Full Sail]]
tail (he stayed in Florida through March 2010, then moved north) and
closes not with a New York event but with a **departure**: the May 2013
return to Uniontown that context-core flags as the timeline's most
load-bearing correction.

## The two apartments and the work

The residence timeline is exact: **424 Bedford Ave (Williamsburg,
Brooklyn), April 2010 – April 2012**, then **Manhattan Upper East Side,
90th between 3rd and Lexington, May 2012 – May 2013**. Professionally
this is the studio-work era — the audio-engineering attempt the Full
Sail degree pointed at, running through **ishlab** (2010–11) and
**Creative License** (2010–2012). It is the one window in Dan's life
where the recording-arts training was actually the job, and it did not
convert into a durable career: the thread simply ends with the 2013
departure, and no audio work of comparable seriousness recurs until the
much later GRIPNOTIC production reactivation.

Alexis was there for all of it — "with me the whole time we were in
nyc," per the Gemini-_58 reconstruction — working at the historic
**Bridge Cafe** (279 Water St, the oldest tavern in the city) until
**Hurricane Sandy** wiped out the Seaport in October 2012, a documented
structural shock to the household economy. A brief 2013 blip with
[[wiki/people/franki-faris|Franki]] ("I really didn't have the romantic
feelings for franki that i said i did at the time") resolved back to
Alexis, foreshadowing the switch-slot mechanics the
[[wiki/mind/synthesis/bond-switch-2015|2015 single-bond analysis]] makes
explicit.

## The two things that started here and did not stop

Two throughlines of the whole corpus originate in this window. The
**Suboxone regimen** begins at its front edge (~January 2010, with a
one-year discrepancy against legacy docs that context-core flags as
resolvable from the first strips reference) — the fifteen-year daily
chassis documented in [[wiki/health/chemical-architecture]] starts here.
And the **Twitter voice** — the weaponized irony and aesthetic
signaling that becomes the linguistic fingerprint — takes its mature
shape in these years, the same online persona that seeds the
"ihatedanfrank" identity later archived in [[wiki/self/facebook]].
Cultural markers of the era are dense in the Facebook likes record: the
chiptune-to-EDM progression (Anamanaguchi → Flying Lotus →
ODESZA/JAUZ), and the **LCD Soundsystem MSG farewell (April 2011)** as a
dated attendance anchor.

## Transition

The period closes with the **May 2013 departure to Uniontown** — the
return that the Twitter record's "2013 systemic rupture" actually marks
(the rupture was the leaving, not a New York event). Everything the old
framing filed under "later NYC" — SLOPPP and MOGZART, the Alexis
collapse, the move to 155 Virginia, the pivot to Annie — belongs to
[[wiki/timeline/periods/uniontown-return-2013-2015]].

**Gaps:** the exact 2010 move date is unresolved (context-core's
residence table starts Brooklyn in April 2010, while an earlier note
claimed a January 3 move — the Florida tail through March 2010 favors
the April date). The Suboxone start year (~2010 vs 2011) is pinned only
from memory, not yet from a first-strips corpus row. No message corpus
survives from this period — the earliest real-text rows in any on-disk
export begin November 12, 2015 — so this page rests on context-core,
the Facebook record, and the Gemini-_58 reconstruction, not on primary
message data.
