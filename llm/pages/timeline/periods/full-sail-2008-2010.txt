# Full Sail (Sep 2008 – Mar 2010)

Three years after the November 2005 family rupture and one year into the
opiate arc ([[wiki/timeline/periods/dark-era-2007-2008]]), Dan left
Uniontown for Winter Park, Florida — Full Sail University, Recording Arts.
This is the hinge period where the recreational/instrumental drug use
stabilizes into permanent chemical maintenance, where the first durable
public voice (Twitter) begins, and where the relationship line pivots
from Danielle to Alexis, the pairing that would eventually lead to [[wiki/people/annie-ulmer|Annie]].

## The degree

An.S. in Recording Arts, graduated August 2009, top 5% of the class — the
formal credential behind the music-production identity that runs through
[[wiki/interests/music/overview]] (SLOPPP, MOGZART, and eventually
GRIPNOTIC all trace back to skills built here). He stayed in Florida
through March 2010 after graduating, a five-month tail before returning
north.

## Twitter genesis

The @danfrank Twitter voice begins here — Pro Tools labs, hip-hop-producer
aspiration, Orlando nightlife, an in-network that already includes
@alexisarmel and @joshbrannan. One specific early post is preserved as a
marker of the period's ideological drift: an explicit "no longer consider
myself a republican" disavowal, the first documented step away from the
Uniontown household's Republican/talk-radio politics
([[wiki/mind/synthesis/political-psyops]] for where that trajectory ends
up).

## The relationship pivot: Danielle ends, Alexis begins

Dan's relationship with [[wiki/people/danielle-onesi]] ends during this
window in Orlando — by his own account, because he cheated. A later
bootloader session names the specific circumstance: a girl from
Baltimore, connected through Jack from the band All Time Low, with
Dan's own regret over the episode explicitly documented. [[wiki/people/alexis-armel]]
begins the same year (2009) and becomes the operative relationship
through the return to New York and beyond, ending only when Annie enters
in November 2015. Alexis wasn't a long-distance partner during this
window — the Twitter corpus places her physically in Orlando with Dan,
tagged directly in local outings ("springs w @alexisarmel," a day trip to
one of the freshwater springs outside the city) and in the same
closed-network in-jokes as [[wiki/people/josh-brannan|Josh Brannan]] and
other Full Sail-era friends, consistent with her later confirmed presence
through the NYC years documented on [[wiki/people/alexis-armel]].

## The friend group

Full Sail seeds the one social cohort in Dan's record that outlasts
every geographic move that follows: [[wiki/people/jason-bermejo|Jason
Bermejo]], [[wiki/people/eric-jester|Eric Jester]],
[[wiki/people/matt-dunn|Matt Dunn]], and
[[wiki/people/jamie-mohler|Jamie Mohler]], a four-person group whose
members are corroborated across two separate 2025 message threads —
Jason's (which names all four) and Eric's (which independently names
Dunn) — decades after Orlando. Jason supplies the era's
music-taste imprint (Kid Cudi, DIY closet-studio recording, blog-era
hip-hop) that runs forward into Dan's whole production identity; the
group's other three members are lower-volume in the corpus but no less
durable — Eric's check-ins resume every year or two with no repair
needed, and Dunn is the subject of an annual $5 birthday joke Eric has
paid without fail for years. Per the operator, Jamie was in New York
with Dan during the NYC-1 years directly following this period and was
present the day Dan first met [[wiki/people/menore|Menore]] — meaning
the introduction to the dealer who would go on to be the most reliable
node of the entire [[wiki/mind/synthesis/supply-network|supply
network]] traces back to this same friend group.

## Pro Tools certification (Jan–Feb 2010)

The Twitter corpus dates the Pro Tools certification push precisely: a
"heavy early-year focus" cluster in January–February 2010, immediately
before the abrupt geographic pivot to NYC (Foursquare check-ins begin
late March 2010 — Brooklyn, DuMont Burger, the Union Ave Walgreens). The
process is documented in Dan's own words across three tweets: "one more
test and i'm a certified pro tools operator!", the triumphant announcement
once it landed — "EAT UR HEART OUT BITCH IM PRO TOOLS CERTIFIED" — and a
wry follow-up admitting the tool he'd actually keep using was a
competitor's: "it's sort of ironic how i finish my pro tools certifications
and go directly to Logic. <3 u, anything made by apple." The credential
became his internship pitch on the move north, self-described plainly as
"recent music engineering grad with pro tools certification" rather than
with any of the tweet-era bravado. (Separately, a later AI-generated
retrospective — Gemini-_58.txt, not a primary source, and stylized as a
dramatized "simulation log" — narrativizes this as a **Pro Tools HD 8**
certification specifically, framing it as advanced signal-routing/TDM/
delay-compensation training that only "the 5% tier" of the graduating
class completed. That level of technical specificity isn't corroborated
anywhere in Dan's own contemporary tweets and should be read as retrospective
narrative coloring, not confirmed fact.) The certification is the credential
that completes the Recording Arts program and is the direct trigger for the
April 2010 move: audio engineering credentials in hand, Dan relocates to
compete in "NYC's density-driven market" rather than stay in Orlando's
post-graduation lull.

## Chemical stabilization

The opiate arc that began in 2007 reaches its resolution at the tail end
of this period: Suboxone initiated around January 2010, with day-zero
later pinned to February 17, 2010 in the fuller chemical-history record
([[wiki/health/chemical-architecture]]) — meaning the single most durable
fact of Dan's adult physiology (sixteen-plus years of daily maintenance,
zero relapses) was set in motion in Winter Park, not Uniontown.

## What comes next

Full Sail ends with the move to Brooklyn in April 2010 — the first of two
distinct New York chapters, this one running to May 2013 before an
Uniontown return that develops the producer aliases and eventually leads
to Annie.

## Gaps

No message corpus or dated record narrows the "Danielle ends, Alexis
begins" sequence to specific weeks within the eighteen-month window; both
transitions are established only at the level of "during Full Sail."
