## Analysis & Connections
~650-659 unique (name parses + stamps). Date range proxies Apr 2009 - Sep 2022 (interaction/activity stamps + known posts; actual "added on" text sparse in clean export). 

Scale: High outward/digital social reach (Uniontown HS/early + NYC recording 2010 + Brooklyn + music festivals/comedy clubs + Pittsburgh return golf/caddie threads + 2020s). Includes family core + scene breadth (music/EDM, comedy, golf).

But: export categories include removed_friends, friends_you_see_less, rejected requests — pruning + algorithmic suggestions ("people you may know"). Visible message depth low (spam heavy in samples). This maps to core **contact-gini** (low primary redundancy, high peripheral count): many ties, few sustained high-fidelity nodes. Annie thread is the exception (deep documented).

**Attachment lens:** Online friend graph as safe/ambiguous tether layer. Volume without proportional exported depth = parallel to pursuit-withdrawal / anxious-preoccupied patterns. Digital connections low-stakes vs. real family/romantic conflict (target story, sister snap in Annie thread).

**Periods tie-in:** Early adds (2009-2011) during post-HS / pre-NYC / early Ishlab; 2010-2015 Brooklyn/NYC music peak (Electric Zoo friends?); 2015+ Annie era + later golf/Pittsburgh re-engagement + unemployment 2020 group. Ongoing low-level into 2022.

**Theorizing:** FB friends list as fossil of contact surface area. High number reflects extroverted / scene-participating phases + early internet social habit (2007-). Cross-ref core contact-gini, attachment-model, periods 2010s + 2017-poverty-floor (network as resource?).
