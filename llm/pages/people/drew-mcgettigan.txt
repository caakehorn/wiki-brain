# Drew McGettigan

Drew McGettigan is a summer-2015 Uniontown supply contact — mostly a
buyer of small quantities from Dan, with one documented go-between deal
in the other direction. The thread runs a tight three months (June–
September 2015) and reads as transactional throughout: a July 22, 2015
exchange coordinates a $30 buy in real time, complete with a stalling
tactic ("I'm gonna meet you here, grab 30, and have you circle the
block twice") and a McDonald's drive-through delay; a September 4
message offers a distinctive, memorably-described batch of MDMA ("it's
crazy. And it's pink... not even like iridescent pink. It looks like
the wallpaper of a Victoria's Secret"). Drew is independently connected
to [[wiki/people/zachariah-harshman|Zach Harshman's]] circle — a July
22 message notes he's "coming in to town soon to meet up with Zach" the
same night — placing him inside the same 2015 Uniontown supply cluster
Zach's own page documents in more depth. A small unresolved thread: Dan
asks his sister Vanessa if she knows Drew, and she confirms she does
"but wouldn't give me any details as to how."
