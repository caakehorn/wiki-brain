# Lewis Strosnider

Lewis Strosnider was a Uniontown vape-shop friend whose Facebook
Messenger thread (March 2018 – February 2019) tracks a genuine
small-business friendship: free graphic design work, a shared drone
business plan, and an unfinished sale that closes exactly when Dan
leaves for New York.

## Design work and the shop

The thread opens with Dan critiquing and redesigning Lewis's business
cards for free ("if you ever need any design help let me know man...
it's a good way to learn some foundational photoshop stuff"), and the
two settle into a genuine customer-friend rhythm around the shop — a
May 2018 e-cig troubleshooting call ends with "just bring it into me
tomorrow, I will mix it up," and Lewis waves off a receipt request with
"Yes sir you know it haha." One August 2018 message, sent to the wrong
thread and immediately caught ("oops wrong thread haha"), reveals Dan
was actively camming that night — "(That's the girl who is camming with
us tonight)" — incidental, real-time confirmation of the smashonista
operation running in parallel with ordinary friendships.

## The drone business that didn't happen

By July 2018 the two are planning to both take the **FAA Part 107**
commercial-drone certification and "build our own commercial business
for viewing buildings for construction companies." Dan floats seed
money from the pending [[wiki/mind/synthesis/estate-money-spine|Fran
estate]] — "in a few months they will wrap up the estate and I'll get
a pretty sizeable check" — an expectation that, per the estate's own
documented timeline, was off by more than two years (the actual
distribution landed September 1, 2020). The drone itself — a **DJI
Mavic 2**, the third of three drones Dan bought that summer (a Mavic
Platinum on July 24, a used unit from
[[wiki/people/david-beard|David Beard]] for $700 around September 4,
then the Mavic 2 itself on September 21, per a separate message to
[[wiki/people/matthew-palermo|Matthew Palermo]], "Got the mavic 2
yesterday") — crashed, paid $800 to repair ($1,600 invested total), becomes
a running sale negotiation from November 2018 through the thread's
final message in February 2019, with Lewis increasingly eager ("I want
it bad...") and Dan noncommittal about price. The sale is still
unresolved when Dan announces, on February 1, 2019, that he'll be
"living in nyc in like 6 weeks" — consistent with, though slightly
later than, the wiki's broader dating of the NYC move to February 2019
— and the thread ends there, mid-negotiation.
