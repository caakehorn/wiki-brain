# Dimitri

Dimitri, the Greek manager of Au Za'atar Midtown, holds a singular
distinction in Dan's entire employment record: "the only boss or manager
or supervisor who i have ever actually really loved." A "perpetual
restaurant manager" and the Triad's oracle on the owners' moods, he
appreciated Dan the way Tarik and Ghassan did "but dimitri was like... a
real person." He is the one who insisted Dan move up from busser to
server — the promotion that created the "Outside Guy" era — and he
brought his own people with him, including
[[wiki/people/ismaila-barry|DJ]]. His first stint lasted only about six
months (per the Volume I storytime) before he quit under the misery of
working for Tarik, then returned ("WITH A STEEL CHAIR," in Dan's
telling) as co-manager alongside the Tunisian zoomer — knowingly
re-entering a job he knew he'd quit again, "choosing the flooded
basement over a quiet life." He likely mediated the recorded Sergio
sit-down. Full context: [[wiki/work/au-zaatar]].

## The iMessage thread

A full two-sided export (handle `+19172925212`, 116 messages,
2023-09-04 to 2024-12-02) resolves a previously flagged data problem —
the earlier extract had appeared entirely inbound, raising the question
of whether Dan ever replied at all. He did, regularly; the direction
field on the earlier extraction was simply unreliable, matching a known
issue across this corpus. The relationship on this channel reads as a
genuine, ongoing friendship that outlasted the working relationship
itself.

The thread ranges widely: casual restaurant-industry shop talk (staffing
churn — "they officially let me go from AZ," a running joke about a
food-delivery robot the restaurant bought, a coworker's move to "some
really fancy French place"), a real argument about ancient history (Dan
pushes that Alexander the Great and the Macedonians weren't "really"
Greek, citing Demosthenes; Dimitri counters at length with linguistic
and cultural evidence, unmoved), a 2024 political exchange where Dimitri
asks Dan directly whether he's excited for Trump's return and Dan
deflects into an anecdote about recording and AI-analyzing an old
argument rather than answering straight, and warm personal notes (a
Piano-learning video Dimitri sends "because it made me think of you";
congratulations on an unspecified accomplishment; Dimitri signing off "I
wish you all the best" ahead of a seven-year-anniversary vacation in
August 2024).

The most consequential exchange is Dan's own account of losing his job:
on August 21, 2024 he tells Dimitri directly that Au Za'atar "officially
let me go," that his former manager Tarik "was really really nice about
it and everything though. We parted on good terms," and that he was
already collecting unemployment and not in immediate financial trouble
from it — a first-person, contemporaneous account of the job loss that
complements the dating already established on
[[wiki/work/au-zaatar]].
