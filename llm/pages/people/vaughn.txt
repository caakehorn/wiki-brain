# Vaughn

Vaughn was a fellow [[wiki/work/nemacolin-caddying|Nemacolin caddie]]
(`+17245578229`, 228 messages, May 2017 – May 2018) who doubled as one
of the smallest documented nodes of the Uniontown
[[wiki/mind/synthesis/supply-network|supply network]]: a weed middleman
without a car, relaying his "homey's" product in $20–50 quantities
between loops. The thread is working-poor Uniontown in miniature —
rides coordinated to collect paychecks, fronts and IOUs in both
directions, product measured in fractions of a gram — and it ends the
way that economy's stories end: "I don't know I start the railroad on
Wednesday" (May 11, 2018), then one last message asking whether Dan
knew "anyone wit a ride that wants to make sum money."

He was a father — deliveries scheduled around "gettin the kids food"
and getting "the kids off the bus at 3" — and an experienced manual
worker who found the caddie yard almost restful: "This is the easiest
job I ever had I'm used to working 12 14 hr days so this a cake walk."
The record is his side only (all rows marked Received); Dan's messages
survive nowhere in it, so every exchange below is reconstructed from
Vaughn's half.

## The yard and the hustle, one channel

The two registers interleave from the first day. Yard logistics: "Do u
loop tomorrow," "Do you have a report time tomorrow," "Brad said ok"
(the caddie master, [[wiki/people/brad-hubeaut|Brad Hubeaut]]),
"I'm going up now to get my check," and the standing carpool problem —
"I'll go up wit you, 1 would be better for me." Supply logistics, same
phone, same days: "Once my homey stops over I'll hit you up," FaceTime
product previews ("I forgot you have a iPhone as soon as I get it I'll
FaceTime you so you can see it"), January 2018's upgrade to
dispensary-sourced flower ("Fire straight from the Dispensary"), oil
carts offered "for the pen" (February 2018), and inventory that never
exceeded pocket scale — "Only got 3 grams left… the best I could do is
50 for all three."

The thread's signature motif is the **short-weight dispute**. Twice Dan
evidently weighed what he'd bought and found it light, and twice Vaughn
retreated to the same defense: "What did it weight because he said it
was 3.5… that's my best friend I got it from and I know he wouldn't do
it on purpose" (May 2017), and "I swear I watched him weigh it" against
a bag holding 0.7 grams (January 2018). Dan owned a scale; Vaughn had to
borrow it ("Whenever you want I just need a scale"). At the market's
bottom tier the middleman couldn't verify his own product — a
quality-control vacuum the network's professional nodes
([[wiki/people/menore|Menore]], [[wiki/people/sam|Sam]]) existed to
solve.

## The February 2018 night

One exchange departs from commerce entirely. Late on February 3, 2018,
Dan evidently shared explicit photos of an unnamed woman; Vaughn's
replies escalate from appraisal ("Hell yeah nipples pierced and
everything") through mock-anguish ("Dan you playing wit my emotions…
I thought we were friends LOL😞😞") to an offer to film ("I got a nice
camera let me tape lol"). Three nights later, after a stood-up supply
run, Vaughn drove over at 1 AM asking "what's up wit me lol" and
reassuring: "Listen you know I'm a complete gentleman / All about
having fun nothing more / I'm here." The thread records nothing further.
The shape — Dan curating an audience, soliciting interest, controlling
access — matches the
[[wiki/mind/psychosexual/orchestration-and-voyeurism|orchestration
pattern]] documented for exactly this period (the arrangement's early
months), but the woman's identity and whether anything occurred are
uncorroborated; the exchange is logged here as pattern-consistent
evidence, not a confirmed instance.
