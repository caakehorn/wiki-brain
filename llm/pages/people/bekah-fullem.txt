# Bekah Fullem

Rebekah "Bekah" Fullem reached out to Dan twice, seven weeks apart, in early 2020 — first in acute crisis over her dying cat and a request for money, then again at the start of the COVID lockdown to disclose she had been raped. The 71-message thread (`+17245629389`, Feb 9 – Mar 29, 2020) is one-sided in tone throughout: Bekah in distress, Dan's responses not preserved in a form that reconstructs clearly from the export.

## February 2020: the cat and the money

Bekah introduces herself as "the girl with the epileptic cat" and over the following two days escalates from a general request for help to explicit financial pleading: her cat, Gideon, needed medication and food she couldn't afford, and without it he would "get put down." She asks for $40–50, apologizes repeatedly for asking, and pushes for payment by a method other than Apple Pay because "the pharmacy doesn't take the card like that." The thread breaks off mid-crisis on February 13 with Bekah saying she'd had to push Gideon's appointment to avoid an immediate euthanasia decision. Whether the money was sent, and what happened to Gideon, is not recorded.

## March 29, 2020: disclosure

A second, single-day exchange six weeks later opens abruptly with Bekah stating "I was raped dude. I haven't slept with anyone since then," and describing a hostile response from law enforcement when she reported it — being asked why the assailant's parents hadn't heard her from across the hall, why she'd had a prior sexual relationship with him, and why she hadn't indicated interest in "that kind of sex." She says the assailant got a lawyer and stalked her, that she lost her job over the fallout, and calls herself "sacrificial lamb." The conversation pivots into the news: Bekah, citing her nursing contacts, insists the just-emerging COVID-19 pandemic in Fayette County is being undercounted and downplayed, pushing back against what reads as Dan's skepticism in the unpreserved half of the exchange. She also mentions her mother has cancer. The thread ends there.
