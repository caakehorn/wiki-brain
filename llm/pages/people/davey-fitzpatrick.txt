# Davey Fitzpatrick

Davey Fitzpatrick was Dan's coworker, then boss, and by the end genuine
friend across the two [[wiki/work/nemacolin-caddying|Nemacolin caddying]]
seasons (`+17249639933`, 382 messages, May 2017 – November 2018): a
fellow caddie in 2017 who came back for 2018 as **assistant caddie
master** — "I'm actually gonna be assistant caddie master lol" — which
made his thread the operational spine of Dan's last Uniontown working
year. Nearly every report time Dan worked that season arrived through
this channel ("8:30 report," "12 report," about twenty of them, March
through October 2018), along with training assignments, float days, and
the affectionate management of a caddie he plainly liked. The thread
ends the day Dan tells him about New York: the final conversation
(November 2, 2018) is Davey absorbing the news — "you guys gonna move
there for a bit? … Wow dude that's serious! Wonder what the cost of
living will be lol … Are you selling your place?" — and signing off with
"Well good luck dude, I wish you guys the best." Dan was in the city
within the week, and the thread never resumes.

The record is his side only (every row marked Received); Dan's voice
survives in the messages Davey reacted to — Likes, Laughs, and Emphasis
quotes — which happen to preserve some of the corpus's better caddie-era
Dan lines ("He's the Pablo Picasso of Mid-Atlantic Polynesian caddies";
"Maybe I'm just so prepared that I memorize the sheet every day so I can
perform background research on my group").

## 2017: peers in the yard

The first season is two caddies talking shop and golfing: rides up the
mountain, loop postmortems, a July round where Davey warns Dan's group
may need to play through because "I have a girl who never golfed before
😂," a Pirates game, and running jokes about the yard's cast —
[[wiki/people/michael-hinkle|Hinkle]] ("I'd pay Hinkle $100 to work our
bags lolz," Dan's line, Davey's laugh), the hashtag "#TKEatsGum," and
above all [[wiki/people/jack-rusko|Jack]], the older caddie whose
neurotic 6:10am ride-shares and single-chasing at dawn both threads
treat as beloved comedy. In October 2017 Davey asks for
[[wiki/people/annie-ulmer|Annie]]'s number for a quick question — the
first sign the friendship extended past the yard — and muses about the
"caddy central" management rumor while insisting "I don't plan on being
there next summer anyway."

## 2018: the report-time channel

He was back by March, promoted, and the thread changes register: Davey
now sets Dan's schedule, covers for him ("Just get here when you can man
if you miss him I'll cover it. You'll work either way"), and recruits
him to train the season's new caddies — "Brooke, Chad, John, maybe Jack"
(May 8), later "I already have you Chad and Luke" — a small but real
mark of standing: the assistant caddie master's pick for teaching the
job was Dan. The scheduling texture preserves the season whole:
pre-dawn singles, "spinning" double-bag days that left everyone "dead,"
gratuity bumped to $30 a man, a wrecked car past the summit Davey warns
Dan around (August 8), and the yard economics of a busy back half —
"Go from hardly working to hardly off lol… But I ain't complaining.
[[wiki/people/trevor|Trev]] picked a bad time to leave" (August 7), the
thread's independent record of Trevor's late-July exit.

Two family threads cross the work channel. On April 5, 2018 — the day
after [[wiki/people/fran-coldren|Fran]] died — Davey texts "Sorry for
your loss bro… If you need anything man let me know," an unprompted
same-week corroboration of the April 4 date the
[[wiki/timeline/events/fran-death-vigil|vigil page]] settled. And Annie
runs through the season constantly: golfing with Dan on June 10 while
carrying her new bag "like a champ" ("She's gonna be working as a caddie
and at the casino soon," Dan reports), and grabbing Dan's phone on July
7 to deliver the thread's warmest joke — "davey !!! its annie, when dan
asked me, i said 'idk i always copied off of davey'" — to Davey's
delight: "That's exactly what I told him you'd say lmao."

## Who he was

Small details accumulate into a person: email self-sent for some
paperwork (February 2018) as `dtfitzpatrick87@gmail.com`; a birthday one
day after Dan's — he answers Dan's November 2 wish with "Thanks my guy
I'm almost as old as you now," dating him a year behind Dan almost to
the day; a mother hospitalized at the end of August 2018 ("all the tests
came back good so far") handled with the same steadiness he ran the
yard with; Morgantown family ties ("caddie ball" and cousins' birthdays
both put him there). His management style is the thread's most
consistent feature — every missed shift answered with some version of
"No worries bud," every favor asked, not ordered.
