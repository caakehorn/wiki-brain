READING RULES
- Each page starts with YAML frontmatter (metadata), then markdown prose.
- [[wiki/domain/page]] is an internal wiki link; its content is at
  https://caakehorn.github.io/wiki-brain/llm/pages/domain/page.txt
- 'connections:' entries in frontmatter are typed, argued relationships
  between pages — treat each claim as a citable conclusion.
- Pages marked knowledge: earned contain reasoned conclusions;
  derived pages are mechanical summaries of source data.
- Start with self/overview and self/context-core for orientation;
  index pages (index.txt files) are navigation hubs per domain.
