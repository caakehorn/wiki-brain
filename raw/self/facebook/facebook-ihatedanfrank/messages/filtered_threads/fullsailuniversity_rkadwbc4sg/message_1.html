Full Sail University
Full Sail Graduate, Danilo Campos, has released version 1.0 of Oddage, an iPhone/iPod Touch game.

In Oddage, players are faced with four words - three that are similar and one that does not belong. Fast brains are rewarded with more points, while the list of possible choices grows.

Included in Oddage are categories as diverse as greek gods, elements from the periodic table, names of countries, ancient civilizations and subatomic particles. Oddage also includes more pedestrian fare, like colors, shapes, trees and spices.

In all, Oddage's library spans over 900 words in 50 categories, ensuring a unique and mentally stimulating experience with every game.
