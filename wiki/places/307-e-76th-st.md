# 307 E 76th St (Manhattan)

307 E 76th St, Upper East Side, was home for the entire second New York
chapter — February 2019 to February 2025 — shared with
[[wiki/people/annie-ulmer|Annie]], and the stage for the relationship's
last whole years and its rupture. The six years at this address contain the
2019 hard-left politicization (Bernie + Chapo), the pandemic bunker years,
the 2020–21 market era ([[wiki/timeline/periods/2020-2021-market-era]]),
the 41-month Au Za'atar job a downtown train ride away
([[wiki/work/au-zaatar]]), the 2024 political-obsession peak, and — in its
final autumn — the concealed [[wiki/people/eli|Eli]] affair whose January 2025 discovery ended
the household ([[wiki/timeline/events/eli-incident]]): Annie was found
unconscious with the coworker in a building lobby ~80 minutes after her
shift, location sharing off. Dan left the address, and New York, on
February 22, 2025.

In the location record the address anchors the NYC cluster (2,301 of 6,227
logged visits — [[wiki/self/location-history]]), and in the identity record
it anchors the "punk as fuck" NYC self-concept ("reasons i moved to nyc: 1.
punk as fuck") that context-core preserves as an identity anchor even after
the return to Pennsylvania. The freezer-phone shibboleth (phone run over on
Second Avenue) belongs to this chapter's geography.
