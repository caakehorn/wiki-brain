# Fastly (FSLY)

Fastly (NYSE: FSLY) is the edge-CDN stock that opened Dan's 2020–2021 market
era. It is not a company he worked for or a person he knew — it is the first
real trade of the period, the tip that tipped him from the sidelines into
active retail trading, and the position that taught him the lesson he later
named: momentum over conviction. The whole arc lives under
[[wiki/timeline/periods/2020-2021-market-era]]; this page holds the FSLY-specific
record.

## How it started — the Jerad tip

The entry was a tip from childhood friend [[wiki/people/jerad-friedline|Jerad
Friedline]], not Dan's own research. Verbatim from the message corpus
(2020-09-20, Received from Jerad):

> "FSLY... busy at the moment but will send my research tomorrow. I made a
> little over $200k from April — now from this stock alone, future of web
> hosting and edge CDN"

> "Amazon is using Fastly on Amazon.com over their own AWS hosting, that's all
> you need to know"

> "Buy FSLY and do not touch for 2-3 years"

Dan bought in. By 2020-09-30 he told Jerad "112 shares my dude" and "fastly
killing it." On 2020-10-06: "dude i made $600 just from FSLY today." He had
dumped roughly $15k of his portfolio into the position and taken a quick ~$4k
profit before the meme-stock eruption widened the game in January 2021.

## The lesson — the big dip

FSLY is where the era's central thesis got burned in. The stock had a sharp
earnings-driven drop in late 2020 (the well-known single-day collapse after a
guidance cut), and Dan read it as a momentum lesson rather than a buy-the-dip
opportunity. Verbatim (2021-02-04, Sent):

> "I learned a lot from that big fastly dip about the importance of momentum
> trading. I never get FOMO about stuff I've sold that has gains later, but you
> feel it hard when you miss a good opportunity to get out"

He was still watching it into 2021: "FSLY earnings tomorrow" (2021-02-17). The
takeaway — sell strength, don't marry the thesis — is the same market-era
insight the operating manual states plainly: cultural-trend sensing had
overtaken fundamentals, and the skill was reading the crowd, not the balance
sheet.

## Why it matters

FSLY is the hinge of the market era: the one tip that converted a pattern-
recognition habit (normally spent on politics, music, relationships) onto
retail markets during the pandemic's "micro-radius siege." It is also the
cleanest instance of the evangelism pattern — Jerad passed it to Dan, Dan
passed it onward to another friend who "started calling in daily gains,"
matching the relational-broadcast instinct documented across the wiki. The
position's modest size (~$15k in, ~$4k profit, a $600 day) is the floor under
the later, larger GME/AMC/CCIV swings.
