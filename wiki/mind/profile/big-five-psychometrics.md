# Big Five / Big30 Psychometrics & PD Vector

The trait-level numbers are the profile's hardest data and its most extreme:
a Big30 facet spread that puts Dan simultaneously in the top decile of the
population for intellect, impulsiveness, liberalism, and self-consciousness
and in the bottom decile for altruism, sociability, assertiveness, modesty,
sympathy, submissiveness, and trust. The dossiers call the combination "the
neurotic genius build." Collapsed to SLOAN notation it reads **RLUEI** —
Reserved, Limbic, Unstructured, Egocentric, Inquisitive. The standing
interpretive rule from context-core: the low social scores are
**architecture, not deficit** — they describe how the system is built, not a
gap between it and its blueprint.

Two structural readings follow from the table. First, the
**intellect/impulsivity paradox**: top-percentile reasoning bolted to
bottom-percentile impulse control is rare and volatile — the deviance audit
scores it 95/100 against baseline — and it explains the corpus pattern of
brilliant analysis coexisting with self-sabotaging execution (the
"diagnosis-to-behavior gap" context-core names as the true failure mode).
Second, the **low-assertiveness surprise**: despite the aggressive analytical
register, Assertiveness sits at 5 and Submissiveness at 1 — he neither leads
nor follows; he exits the vertical axis entirely, which is the psychometric
root of [[wiki/mind/synthesis/vertical-authority-skepticism]].

## RLUEI, facet by facet

**Reserved.** Intense introversion; days can pass without speech; people are
draining except in rare vetted cases; "general detachment from day-to-day
affairs." Matches the GPS record's home-anchoring (0.68) and the Gini-0.961
contact concentration ([[wiki/mind/concepts/contact-gini]]).

**Limbic.** High anxiety, mood swings, depressive undertow — emotionally
reactive despite the intellectual veneer, oscillating between cold
detachment and intense anguish, with logic recruited to justify
hopelessness. Chronic worst-case rumination; emotional "weather systems"
managed through intellectual frameworks rather than release.

**Unstructured.** Disorganized, schedule-resistant, minimal weight on
cleanliness or comfort; the workspace-as-chaos-nest; obsessive *internal*
logical structure maintained inside external disorder; work in intense
bursts followed by collapse. The documented vampire schedule (peak output
~11 PM–4 AM) is this facet plus self-preservation instinct.

**Egocentric.** Own intellectual goals prioritized over others' needs; low
agreeableness; direct to the point of rudeness; not malicious — focused
inward to the exclusion of the external. The record's version: "harm caused
is collateral from indifference, not malice."

**Inquisitive.** Hyperfixation as the primary mode of engagement. The
documented cycle: discovery (an obscure topic with sufficient mystery
captures total attention) → consumption (days-to-months immersion, sleep
lost, resources devoured, 45-minute unprompted monologues) → synthesis
(original creative or analytical output) → abrupt abandonment once the
mystery is solved, then the next fixation. Corpus examples: Roman Republic
(hard stop at Augustus), J6 raw footage, market trading, AI agent pipelines,
this wiki.

([[wiki/mind/profile/deviance-mapping]],

intelligence estimate in the 98th–99th percentile with "exceptional fluid
reasoning, verbal comprehension, and pattern recognition."

## What the numbers predict — and did

The facet spread predicts, in advance, most of the documented life record:
single-node attachment with catastrophic failure on node loss (Sociability
3 + sx instinct → [[wiki/mind/concepts/attachment-model]]); refusal of
vertical claims at any scale (Trust 9, Submissiveness 1 →
[[wiki/work/bfs-foods]]); market-era risk appetite and the 2015 impulse
theft (Impulsiveness 96 → [[wiki/legal/2015-retail-theft-arrest]]);
encyclopedic curated taste (Artistic Interests 81, Inquisitive →
[[wiki/interests/favorites/index]]); and the hard-left politics (Liberalism
91 → [[wiki/mind/synthesis/political-psyops]]). The profile's value to the
wiki is exactly this predictive compression: one table that most of the
behavioral corpus instantiates.

**Gaps:** no instrument name, norm group, or administration date for the
Big30 or PD screens; scores appear identically across dossiers, suggesting a
single unrecorded test session; no post-2026 retest.
