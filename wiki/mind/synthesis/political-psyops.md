# Political Psyops & Hybrid Events

Dan's political skepticism is not partisan; it is the forensic default
applied to institutions on every side. A democratic socialist with zero
Trump or MAGA loyalty, he treats politics as both near-total ideological
pursuit and aesthetic spectacle — and he deploys that non-loyalty

clearest single-event crystallization of the method is January 6th, which
the behavioral spine records as CONCLUDED: a hybrid of organic riot and
intelligence co-opt.

## The J6 hypothesis

The claim is deliberately narrow. Not that the rally was staged or the
event fake — but that a small operational insertion rode on organic
conditions to manufacture an optical product: martyrdom imagery and
revolutionary-violence branding designed to poison the populist right's
future electoral chances.

The anomaly set, assembled from raw-footage study, spans four categories.
Ballistics: the Ashli Babbitt shooting produced a non-exiting torso shot
with a contained energy dump (hemothorax) rather than the arterial spray of
"Hollywood physics" — consistent with forensic pathology, inconsistent with
the initially reported geometry — and the shooter went unidentified for
months. Procedure: no perimeter was established after lethal force in a
volatile scene, medical intervention was cosmetic (unzipping a jacket), and
officers intermingled with the crowd rather than securing it; SWAT
stepped aside at the critical choke point. Crowd behavior: the group
ignored an open door feet from the Senate chamber while Romney evacuated
through it; fight footage resembles a "sad mosh pit"; police cuffed and
released the Proud Boy who smashed the window. Documentation: the sole
clean footage of the shooting came from John Sullivan, an anti-Trump
activist positioned perfectly to capture it.

The Gemini activity archive (Jan 2, 2026 cluster) formalizes this as the
"Operation Wildfall" thesis: a "Sandwich" operation — an organic outer
layer with an inserted lethal core, Babbitt as "narrative necessity" and
"Psy-Kill" rather than tactical threat ("she was a prop": unarmed, small,
white, female veteran — cast for optics), under an "inverted containment"
doctrine where the outer riot stays porous while the inner scene is

the anomaly pile is Gish Gallop and genuine trauma-weirdness (stress

was the imagery; the execution didn't need to be perfect," because almost
