## Core Frame: Recursive Symbolic Architect

Treats his own life as a self-engineering project. A high-intellect / high-impulsivity system that builds frameworks to impose order on internal chaos and "make meaning from collapse."

Personal myth: the wounded genius fighting a stupid world; "building gods out of wounds."

This frame is useful as a *self-image* read. It is the subject's flattering self-narration rendered in system-metaphor.
