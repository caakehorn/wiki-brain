# Dan's Law

**Source:** [[wiki/self/context-core]] §6

**Definition:** When a coincidence cluster requires every element to be simultaneously innocent and the joint probability is near-zero, assume real signal exists — but treat 1–2 elements as parasitic noise pattern-matched onto it. Find the irreducible load-bearing element → strip the gravity-well attachments → what remains is the signal.

Distinct from:
- Full conspiracy-collapse (all elements load-bearing).
- Skeptic-dismissal (one explained element kills the entire cluster).

## Applications in Sources

**J6 (hybrid co-opt hypothesis):**
- Cluster: Babbitt shooting anomalies (identification delay, geometry), immediate procedural violations (no perimeter, cosmetic medical), door blocker (directed), cuff/release, ignored Senate door during Goodman chase, informant presence in groups, perfect optical outcome (martyrdom + revolutionary imagery).
- Joint probability of all innocent under pure chaos model is extremely low.
- Load-bearing: "The point was the imagery." Small operational insertion after organic conditions.
- Parasitic noise stripped: Some crowd behavior may be emergent; focus on specific directed elements that require function/knowledge.
- Outcome per chat + core: "CONCLUDED — hybrid organic event + intelligence co-opt."

**Drawer Shortage (BFS):**
- Cluster: CSR clean close report vs next-day personal "you owe me" claim, zero paper trail, schedule bump, single-witness, PA law bypass.
- Low probability all innocent under normal accounting.
- Load-bearing: Absence of trail is the feature (no recourse for either party). Suggests either cover or extraction.
- Parasitic: Exact $50 number origin may contain recount error; the off-books demand remains the signal.
- Tactical: Do not validate by paying.

## Relation to Core Axioms and Method
- Directly operationalizes "find where it breaks" and hinge-instant focus.
- Supports "not vigilant = annihilated" axiom.
- Used in political nodes (zero-sum structures, electoral mechanics as "coup" despite legality).
- Cross-domain: same law applied to relationship gaslighting clusters (prior dox) and institutional behavior.

## Evidence Handling
- Abundance of circumstantial anomalies is necessary but not sufficient.
- Must distinguish elements that require coordination/knowledge vs. those chaos can generate.
- "Abundance of circumstantial issues" threshold crossed in J6 chat leading to hybrid conclusion.
