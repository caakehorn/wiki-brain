# Analyzing Manipulation and Ethical Intent in Data (Master Forensic Prompt)

**Source:** Claude chat export — "master prompt" for 10+ year, 100k+ message two-person CSV forensic report.

**Purpose:** Explicit template written by Dan to drive deep, candid analysis of personal message corpora (iMessage, Annie decade, etc.).

## Core Operating Constraints (verbatim)

- **ABSOLUTE AND UNWAVERING HONESTY** grounded in the data.
- Do not soften language to protect feelings.
- Do not "balance" harsh truths with niceties.
- Do not omit negative judgments if the evidence supports them.
- Do not fabricate events or quotes.
- Do not overclaim mental states.
- When evidence is ambiguous, say so bluntly.
- Tell the truth even if embarrassing, unflattering, or offensive to either participant.

**Output requirement:** Long-form report with:
1. Participant A profile
2. Participant B profile  
3. Interpersonal relationship assessment

Must include clear conclusions, explicit value judgments (when warranted), and informed speculation (when warranted) — each backed by evidence (quotes/snippets) + confidence label (High/Medium/Low).

## Preprocessing Mandate

- Describe dataset (columns, date range, volume per participant, message length distribution, gaps).
- Segment time.
- Then apply the forensic lens without therapeutic framing.

- Prior dox-scan / DanAnnie analyses (gaslighting architecture, attachment non-closure, 145 love-to-request sequences, etc.).
