- Immediate response anomalies: no perimeter, no real CPR/medical (cosmetic actions like unzipping jacket), police intermingling with crowd without securing scene.
- Crowd behavior: in one stream, group moves but ignores open door near Senate chambers (Romney evacuating); "fight" footage looks like "sad mosh pit"; non-chalant "door blocker" preventing entry to key area.
- Police cuffing and then releasing Proud Boy who smashed window allowing entry.
- Historical precedent: COINTELPRO, FBI informants in Oath Keepers/Proud Boys (trial testimony), agent provocateurs.
- Optical result too perfect for pure chaos: martyrdom for one side, violence imagery for other.
- "The point was the imagery. The execution didn't need to be perfect" because few study raw footage with open mind; classified as "fringe" dismisses details.

Dan notes loneliness of position: not fitting left (needs pure fascist coup) or right (needs total innocent or full fed setup) narratives. "Abundance of circumstantial anomalies" warrants investigation that won't happen.
