# Lucie Dobbin

Lucie Dobbin was a longtime friend of [[wiki/people/fran-coldren|Fran
Coldren]] from her winters in Florida — a "friend from Quebec City,"
per the thread's own account of a shared birthday party years earlier —
who received, within hours of Fran's death, the richest first-person
account Dan ever wrote of that night. He reached out to her rather
than to family: "you're one of the people who already knew just how
magical that woman was."

## The death, in Dan's own words

On April 5, 2018 — the evening after Fran died — Dan messaged Lucie
with an account not preserved in this detail anywhere else in the
corpus. Annie had fallen asleep at the bedside during the third day of
Fran's catatonic hospital stay; alone and awake, Dan tried, half as an
experiment, "communicating with gram" using only his mind — "it was a
silly idea, and something i never expected to produce a result...but
right when i asked her if she could hear me: she woke up." He woke
Annie; both told Fran they loved her; she smiled, went quiet, smiled
once more, and died — "it was beautiful and peaceful and she was truly
happy." The hospital's pronouncement followed within a minute, audible
on the phone recording Dan made of the moment. Writing as "completely
non-religious" and "skeptical about most supernatural situations," he
allows only that the experience "was so powerful and so 'human' that i
will need to process just how differently the world might be than i
had originally thought" — a rare register of open uncertainty, offered
to Lucie specifically because he judged she would understand it without
needing to be convinced. Lucie's reply describes [[wiki/people/vicki|Vicki]] — Fran's caretaker — independently rewatching the video Dan
sent, saying she "would have had trouble accepting it if she hadn't
seen with her own eyes that she was okay."

## Who she was to Fran

Lucie is identified in the thread as "the granddaughter of Fran's
next-door neighbors and close friends Jim and Sugie Ulmer" — placing
her, like Annie, inside the extended Belmont Circle/Ulmer social
world, though not as a blood relation of Annie's own immediate family.
Dan credits Lucie and her partner Chris by name for enabling Fran's
continued winter trips to Florida late in her life: "without you and
chris doing so much... she wouldn't have been able to continue doing
it," adding that the household kept telling Fran "she had just gotten
back recently" rather than that the trips were ending, to spare her the
grief of losing that independence. The thread also names "Genie"
(spelled "Jeanie" by Lucie) as another Florida-era friend of Fran's,
connected to the Dobbins by marriage — a detail Dan says he only pieced
together while going through Fran's old photographs after her death.

Dan invited Lucie and Chris to a memorial celebration at the Uniontown
Country Club on May 6, 2018 (12–4 PM), explicitly framed as "a happy
occasion" rather than a funeral; Lucie's reply suggests they were still
in Florida and uncertain whether they could attend. The thread's last
entry is a Facebook friendship-anniversary notice in August 2019; there
is no message after April 2018.
