# Frances "Fran" Thomas Whyel Coldren

## Identity
Maternal great-grandmother (Whyel line) of Dan Frank. Born 15 August 1920, Fort Martin, West Virginia. Died 4 April 2018, Uniontown, Fayette County, Pennsylvania. Self-described as Dan's **biggest life influence**. Dirt-poor WV origins; coal-baron marriage; later third husband a 33rd-degree Mason. Reportedly dated Arnold Palmer. Gifted the Numark NS7 DJ controller to Dan.

> **[AGE DISCREPANCY — verify against primary source]:** Context-core says "d. April 2018, age 98." GEDCOM primary source gives birth date Aug 15, 1920, which makes her 97 at April 4, 2018 death (she would have turned 98 in August 2018). Either (a) the 1920 birth date is correct and she died at 97, or (b) her birth year was 1919 and the GEDCOM has an error. The wiki used 1920 per the GEDCOM. User should verify against Herald-Standard obituary or FindAGrave which may carry the definitive year.
