# Lukyan Mraz

Lukyan Mraz is a Uniontown friend, connected to the same household as
[[wiki/people/zachariah-harshman|Zach Harshman]], whose short Facebook
Messenger thread (36 messages, August 2015 – March 2016) documents a
brief cannabis-cultivation collaboration that ends, seven months later,
in a tense dispute over borrowed equipment.

## The grow attempt

In August 2015 Lukyan lends Dan what the two describe, half in code,
as "a book... on lap quilting" — a growing guide, given the context:
Dan calls it "super helpful" and "all you need to know in one place,"
Lukyan jokes it's "been in my family for generations... groovy," and
the exchange turns explicitly toward setting something up: "if you
want to set up something bigger I can help out. I have a bunch of
equipment, but no really good space. I'd love to start something
again, and I like teaching it." A September 23, 2015 update — "Confirmed
female on the first one" — is the thread's only signal the project
actually produced anything (a female cannabis plant, in a small home
grow, is the desired outcome). Late-August messages have Lukyan
checking whether Zach was at the house before stopping by, placing him
inside the same social circle documented on Zach's own page.

## The ending

The friendship's last exchange, seven months later, sours completely.
On February 17, 2016 — one day before Zach separately asks Dan "is
everything alright with Luke?" in his own thread — Lukyan sends "Hey
dude you alright?", suggesting some kind of trouble or falling-out of
mood was already visible from outside. By March 11, 2016 the register
has turned hostile: "When should I expect you? Because I am expecting
you... Sunrise is at 6:37, almost out of time," followed by an
ultimatum — "I have better shit to do than play Dan's games. He said
he was bringing everything last night. You have 12 hours. Midnight or
I'll report it stolen." The thread ends there; whatever was owed and
whether it was returned is undocumented.
