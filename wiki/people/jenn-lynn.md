# Jenn Lynn

Jenn Lynn is a Uniontown-area acquaintance whose three-year Facebook
Messenger thread (August 2017 – September 2020) runs two tracks in
parallel: a recurring, never-quite-resolved solicitation for a paid or
swap sexual arrangement involving Jenn's boyfriend **Brad**, and an
ordinary, more consistently-followed-through drug-sourcing friendship.

## The arrangement offers

Dan first raises it February 8, 2019, unprompted and in the same
register as his other paid-companion outreach: "me and Annie have been
trying to find a nice 'friend' that would want to be paid well to hang
out with me/us... $500-700 depending on the girl." Jenn asks whether he
"need[s] a girl or need[s] to pimp one," and the conversation pivots,
once Brad enters it, from a straight paid booking into a proposed
two-couple swap — Dan is explicit that a swap "takes the money out of
play." Jenn agrees in principle ("Yea let's do it") but the night
dissolves into logistics and doesn't resolve on the page. A second, more
concrete attempt runs October 11–12, 2019 — timed to a trip home with
his mother, Suz, present at the house — with both sides confirming
readiness ("We're ready to get freaky!!"), though the thread never
explicitly confirms the encounter happened. A third attempt, September
9–11, 2020, revives the same swap framing ("Brad's been wanting to watch
someone bang the shit outta me!!... we can swap. I don't mind") and
again trails off without confirmation. Throughout, Dan frames Annie's
involvement as pre-cleared ("she lets me do what I want," "I do have
permission tho") and is candid, in the 2019 messages, about the
couple's camming operation as a related but separate track — sending
[[wiki/people/annie-ulmer|Annie]] cam-show clips and directing Jenn to
their Chaturbate handle, "smashonista." The pattern —
uncompensated-friend solicitation running parallel to and shading into
the couple's commercial camming — is the same shape as the documented
[[wiki/people/shelbie-breakiron|Shelbie Breakiron]] instance on
[[wiki/mind/psychosexual/arrangement-history|the arrangement-history
page]], but with a boyfriend explicitly in the mix from the start rather
than added later.

## The drug-sourcing thread

Running underneath and outlasting the sexual solicitation is a genuine,
reciprocal supply relationship: Dan repeatedly asks Jenn to source
psilocybin mushrooms, MDMA, or LSD ("acid" / "cid" — Jenn: "I took some
a cpl weeks ago and it was fantastic. Old dead head shit"), while he
brings kratom and other supply back from his NYC/travel trips for her
("I have SO much of it"). The September 2020 exchange has Dan asking her
to drive him home from a trip in exchange for "drugs and money," and
separately to ride along to NYC and drive a rental car back — offers she
declines both times, citing work.
