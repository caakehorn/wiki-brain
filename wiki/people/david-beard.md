# David Beard

David Beard is a golf-course-adjacent Uniontown contact — his own
messages reference "work" rain delays and a Tim Griglack's band hangout
"down behind number 5 on course below your home," placing him inside
the same Nemacolin/Belmont Circle geography as Dan's caddying network
— whose short but transactionally dense thread (July–October 2018)
documents the middle purchase in a rapid three-drone summer.

## The middle drone

On July 24, 2018, Dan tells David — in confidence, "because I will be
chastised for irresponsible spending" — that he'd just bought a **DJI
Mavic Platinum**. David, unprompted, offers to sell his own used drone
instead: "I was thinking about seeing if you wanted to buy mine,
seriously." The negotiation runs for six weeks — an initial $800 ask,
David's insistence Dan test-fly it first ("please see what i have, use
it then decide"), a Aug 20 discount to $750, and a final $700 close on
September 3–4, 2018, with Dan reporting back the next morning: "This
thing is awesome. I love it. I'm glad I didn't spend twice as much on
the new one." Eighteen days later, on September 21–22, 2018, Dan buys a
**third** drone — the DJI Mavic 2 — telling both David ("I caved and
bought the mavic 2. It's so freakin cool") and, separately,
[[wiki/people/matthew-palermo|Matthew Palermo]] the same week. The Mavic
2 is the unit that becomes the unresolved $1,600 sale saga documented
on [[wiki/people/lewis-strosnider|Lewis Strosnider's]] page months
later — meaning Dan owned or had owned three separate drones inside a
single 2018 summer.
