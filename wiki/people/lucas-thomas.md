# Lucas Thomas

Lucas Thomas is a mutual friend of [[wiki/people/alexis-armel|Alexis Armel]]'s
post-breakup boyfriend, Zac Shumar, known to the wiki through a single, dense
five-day Facebook Messenger exchange in February 2017 — not a friendship of
his own with Dan, but the corpus's only **contemporaneous, real-time**
account of the arrest that later sent Alexis to prison. Full account of the
event itself is at
[[wiki/people/alexis-armel#post-breakup-the-zac-shumar-arrest-and-incarceration-2017|Alexis
Armel's Zac Shumar section]]; this page exists to record Lucas himself and
what only his thread carries.

## The two messages

Lucas messages Dan cold on February 11, 2017 — the two apparently were not
previously in direct contact — to report that Lex's place had caught fire
and he can't reach her; he'd gotten Dan's contact from "one of her friends."
Five days later, at 5am on February 16, Dan reaches back out first, unprompted,
to tell Lucas not to call Zac. The conversation that follows is the
corpus's most granular account of the arrest: Zac picked up mail at a man
named "RT"'s house, RT had reportedly been "hanging out with some talkative
people, who may or may not have been getting government salaries," and
fifteen police were staged to swarm when Zac took the package — both men
read it as a probable informant tip rather than coincidence. Lucas supplies
the detail that a glass smoking rig Zac had loaned him for the Philadelphia
trip was seized along with everything else in Zac's car, and that Zac's
parents learned about the arrest and the house fire in the same phone call
from jail, having not been told about the fire beforehand. Lucas also
surfaces a detail no other source in the corpus carries: their mutual
friend "Jericho" asked Lucas to notify Zac's Philadelphia roommates that he
wouldn't be back for a while — evidence Zac had an independent
Philadelphia life in progress at the time of the arrest, cut short by it.
