# Aaron

Aaron is a short-lived but substantive iMessage contact (`+17245624801`,
38 messages, August 21 – September 13, 2025) whose thread's real value
is a single real-time exchange: Dan's own reaction to the September 11,
2025 assassination of Charlie Kirk, delivered the same day.

## The Charlie Kirk exchange

Aaron opens directly — "Hey, what are your thoughts on the Charlie Kirk
shooting?" — and Dan is unhesitant and specific: "I mean I don't think
killing CHARLIE KIRK solves anything or makes much sense... But I'm not
upset about it." He pushes back on an emerging claim about the
ammunition ("I do not believe they found bullets with 'trans slogans'
on them... Wtf is a trans slogan"), reads the political fallout as
minimal because of who Kirk was rather than despite it — "he wasn't
really much of an ideologue. He's about as middle-of-the-road, vanilla
milquetoast MAGA conservative there is... there's not much political
capital to gain here" — and Aaron agrees. Asked whether Kirk's
supporters might riot, the exchange doesn't resolve the question
directly. The register matches the "sicko" justice-system skepticism
already documented from Dan's Twitter activity around the earlier Luigi
Mangione/UnitedHealthcare-CEO killing (see
[[wiki/self/twitter|Twitter]]) — opposition to political violence in
principle, paired with an unsentimental read of who benefits from it.

## Before that: Mangione and JFK

The thread's earlier content, from late August 2025, is warmer and more
casual — Aaron responding well to a video Dan shared connecting the
JFK assassination and Watergate to George H.W. Bush, and separately
reacting to the Mangione case directly: "Yes, Luigi is also hot!!,"
paired with an emphasized quote from the shared video framing the
UnitedHealthcare CEO's killing as a proportionate response to systemic
healthcare injustice ("we should be building statues for someone who
chose to value turning that dude's skull into pink mist over his own
freedom"). Aaron also shares small personal texture unprompted — audiobook
and Harry Potter enthusiasm — and the two discuss a trip to Morgantown.
