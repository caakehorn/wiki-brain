# Jay Lauer

Jay Lauer was a friend and mutual suboxone/cocaine trading partner over a seven-week stretch spanning the 2016–2017 New Year (`+17248121368`, 116 messages, Dec 23, 2016 – Feb 11, 2017). Unlike the purely mechanical dealer threads elsewhere in the corpus, this one has real friction and real friendship texture: Jay's finances are controlled by a partner named Betherin, he repeatedly comes up short and asks Dan to front him, and the two clearly hang out beyond the transactions — visiting each other's homes, planning to bring an acoustic guitar over, referencing a shared friend group and a New Year's Eve gathering at "Claire's."

## Texture

Jay's messages track a familiar addiction-economy pattern: promising payment "when my check clears," scraping together partial amounts ($10, $20, $30–40 at a time), and repeatedly asking Dan to "look out" for him even after running out of money — "I need some bad man.. but I blew all my money under the impression you would look out." A January 19, 2017 exchange takes a dark turn when Jay, apparently joking about a physical confrontation over sunglasses, escalates into an offensive 9/11 joke ("I bet your a fuckin Muslim an I'm gonna beat you Just like the heroes did on flight 93... God bless"), then immediately walks it back ("Sorry I got carried away") — a jarring but characteristic register-swing for the thread. Political commentary (anti-Trump, impeachment talk) appears briefly in late January 2017. The thread ends abruptly on February 11, 2017 with an unanswered "Yo."
