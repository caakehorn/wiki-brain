# David J. Frank

## Identity
Paternal great-grandfather of Dan Frank. Born 12 August 1892 in Russia (Jewish immigrant). Died 6 April 1960, Brownsville, Fayette County, Pennsylvania. Married Sadie Harris (b. Austria 1900). Father of Morley Jay Frank.

## Timeline
- **1892-08-12**: Born, Russia.
- **Early 1900s**: Immigration to US (Eastern European Jewish wave).
- **1930**: Brownsville, Fayette Co., PA (census context).
- **1960-04-06**: Death, Brownsville, PA.
