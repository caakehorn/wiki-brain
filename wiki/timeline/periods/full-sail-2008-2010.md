# Full Sail (Sep 2008 – Mar 2010)

Three years after the November 2005 family rupture and one year into the
opiate arc ([[wiki/timeline/periods/dark-era-2007-2008]]), Dan left
Uniontown for Winter Park, Florida — Full Sail University, Recording Arts.
This is the hinge period where the recreational/instrumental drug use
stabilizes into permanent chemical maintenance, where the first durable
public voice (Twitter) begins, and where the relationship line pivots
from Danielle to Alexis, the pairing that would eventually lead to [[wiki/people/annie-ulmer|Annie]].

## The degree

An.S. in Recording Arts, graduated August 2009, top 5% of the class — the
formal credential behind the music-production identity that runs through
[[wiki/interests/music/overview]] (SLOPPP, MOGZART, and eventually
GRIPNOTIC all trace back to skills built here). He stayed in Florida
through March 2010 after graduating, a five-month tail before returning
north.

## Twitter genesis

The @danfrank Twitter voice begins here — Pro Tools labs, hip-hop-producer
aspiration, Orlando nightlife, an in-network that already includes
@alexisarmel and @joshbrannan. One specific early post is preserved as a
marker of the period's ideological drift: an explicit "no longer consider
myself a republican" disavowal, the first documented step away from the
Uniontown household's Republican/talk-radio politics
([[wiki/mind/synthesis/political-psyops]] for where that trajectory ends
up).

## The relationship pivot: Danielle ends, Alexis begins

Dan's relationship with [[wiki/people/danielle-onesi]] ends during this
window — by his own account, because he cheated. [[wiki/people/alexis-armel]]
begins here and becomes the operative relationship through the return to
New York and beyond, ending only when Annie enters in November 2015.

## Chemical stabilization

The opiate arc that began in 2007 reaches its resolution at the tail end
of this period: Suboxone initiated around January 2010, with day-zero
later pinned to February 17, 2010 in the fuller chemical-history record
([[wiki/health/chemical-architecture]]) — meaning the single most durable
fact of Dan's adult physiology (sixteen-plus years of daily maintenance,
zero relapses) was set in motion in Winter Park, not Uniontown.

## What comes next

Full Sail ends with the move to Brooklyn in April 2010 — the first of two
distinct New York chapters, this one running to May 2013 before an
Uniontown return that develops the producer aliases and eventually leads
to Annie.

## Gaps

No message corpus or dated record narrows the "Danielle ends, Alexis
begins" sequence to specific weeks within the eighteen-month window; both
transitions are established only at the level of "during Full Sail."
