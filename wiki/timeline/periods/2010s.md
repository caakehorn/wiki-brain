# Early 2010s (NYC Round One)

## Date range

January 2010 through late 2014 — the stretch from the Brooklyn move to the start of the Annie relationship. Bookended by graduation from Full Sail (Recording Arts, 2010) on one side and the Thanksgiving-2015 relationship start on the other.

## Defining characteristics

Dan's first New York City period: the January 3, 2010 move to Brooklyn, the ishlab and Creative License audio-engineering jobs (2010–2012), and the five-year relationship with [[wiki/people/alexis-armel]] (Alexis Armel, met through webcam work). This is the pre-Annie, pre-Uniontown-return era — professionally the audio-career attempt, socially the early Facebook network peak, and the origin of the "ihatedanfrank" persona documented in [[wiki/self/facebook]]. Suboxone maintenance begins in February 2010. The detailed early-career reconstruction lives in [[wiki/self/chats/gemini-58]].

## Data profile

The Facebook corpus is densest here for network growth (friend adds run heavily 2009–2014) and for the music-taste record — the chiptune-to-EDM progression in the likes data (Anamanaguchi → Flying Lotus → ODESZA/JAUZ) tracks this window. Several peripheral message-corpus contacts date their only real activity to this period: short bursts with people who never became sustained threads.
