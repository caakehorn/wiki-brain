## Legal Context
Pennsylvania Wage Payment and Collection Law (43 P.S. § 260.3): Employers cannot deduct for shortages without specific written authorization; cannot take below min wage. Off-books demand bypasses payroll protections. No paper trail = feature (no recourse). FLSA anti-kickback (29 CFR § 531.35) inchoate (no completed payment). Unemployment compensation (partial UC) stronger lever.
